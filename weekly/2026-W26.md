## 📚 Research Deep Dive: Orchestration

This week's research emphasizes that **orchestration is where enterprise value is created**, not just model quality. Key aspects:

1. **Context Management**: Ensuring the right data reaches the right agent at the right time
2. **Error Handling**: Resilient failure recovery and escalation protocols
3. **Multi-Provider Support**: Combining best-in-class models from different vendors
4. **Component Swapping**: Ability to update agents as landscape evolves

**Papers to Read:**
- **MegaFlow**: Large-scale distributed orchestration for 10K+ concurrent agents
- **JudgeFlow**: Block-level responsibility scoring for workflow optimization
- **POLARIS**: Typed planning and governed execution for back-office automation
